Class project

Objective: Create a culturally significant sculpture using Minecraft.

Dia de los Muertos is an important cultural holiday to me. It is a day to remember and celebrate our loved ones who have past away. I wanted to not only use the most recognized part of it (the decorated skull) but also the part that means the most to me (the Ofreta). I love everything about the holiday: the offerings, the ofreta, the clothing. It reminds me of home. This year will be the first year I won't be celebrating it with my family, and I can't use candles in the dorm room. Instead, I made my own in Minecraft. I encourage people, if they have a loved one who has past away, to put a sign in my sculpture with their name, so we can celebrate them together.
