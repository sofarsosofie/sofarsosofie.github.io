Storyteller

My main goal through my art is to tell a story. Stories are what ties humanity together. They are a part of us when we are young and when we pass on, stories will be told of us. A movie, a novel, or a comic is nothing without a powerful story. It is my dream to be able to not only capture someone else's story, but also express my own.
