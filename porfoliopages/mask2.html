This is another type of self-portrait I was experimenting with. The figure in the drawing is me or a "perfect" version of me. For years I was called a doll because of my size and features. The sphere with the marigolds is a mask. It represents the mask I wear to the world. A teacher once told me that I hid behind a mask "covered in glitter and flowers." By making my mask in photoshop, I acknowledge the existence and meaning of the mask. This artwork was a way for me to explore who I am and who I want to be to the world.
