My name is Maeva and I am a Eschrichtius robustus or a Gray Whale for short.

I am four years old now. I have a Mom and a Dad who always look after me.

We travel with our pod so I guess there is a lot of us looking after each other.

Ever since I was born we have been around Mexico and California. But this year is different.

We are migrating farther than we ever have in a very long time. A huge group of us are going to Washingtion and I

am super excited! Mom says its the prettiest place she has ever seen! That's why I want to celebrate with all of you!

Fun Facts About My Family

My is about 15 meters long! Thats about 45 feet! I'm only 6 meters long but I hope one day to be as big as him

My mom is the toughest person I know! No one can mess with her! I want to be like her when I grow up.

We are Eastern Gray whales which means we are local to the west coast of Central and North America.

There used to be not many of us. Mom and Dad won't tell me why yet though. But there is so many more of us now!

We also love to sing! If you come to the celebration, maybe we will even sing for you guys!
