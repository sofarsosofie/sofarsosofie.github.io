Hey internet,

My name is Tristan and I'm from the Makah Tribe. I live in Neah Bay in Washington. I have a little brother named Kai.

I'm nine years old but I'll be turning ten next month.

This is the beginning of my blog for my summer homework. We're supposed to keep a journal of our everyday life.

We get extra credit if we do more that just "write it down."

Anyway, today has been hectic, more hectic than usual. Kai kept trying to run away while we were going to the park. I swear that kid is going to get me in so much trouble

Dad keeps talking about the renewing of a tradition. Its been 70+ years

since we were able to celebrate it. I guess I'm not sure how I feel about it yet. But I guess I have to know more about it...Will post updates.

Before I forget, we are supossed to have an image after our entry.
