Tristan's blog

August 24, 2018

Our fanily is having a huge celebration! I haven't seen everyone this excited for so long.

But I can't help but feel a little sad. A whale got hit by a fishing boat yesterday. Kai cried when he found out. He loves whales a lot

Our tribe gets to keep the whale though. My dad went to help cut up the whale for a feast. He says the whale is going to feed all of us for weeks too!

He says he's content because we can celebrate our culture in this way. And its just in time for Makah Days too.

I'm happy that I can be a part of it too.

I hope one day people will understand our way of life, because we want to celebrate every part of our culture.
